No Salt Sailing is a nonprofit organization that provides veterans and their families with the opportunity to enjoy sailing and develop maritime skills at no cost. They leverage a fleet of sailing vessels and expert instructors to develop new skills create community, resilience, and joyful experiences in the Chicago area on Lake Michigan.

This initiative is spearheaded by an Army veteran who is on his own journey of healing and recovery following his challenging military service.

This is an ongoing pro bono project that aligns with my membership in the veteran community, my passion for sailing, and my commitment to promoting access to the recreational benefits of Lake Michigan.
The existing branding lacked cohesion, emotional resonance and scalability.

Key issues include:
  • An outdated logo and inconsistent visual identity.
  • No formal design system for digital and print communications.
  • A website that was difficult to update and visually disconnected from the organization's mission.

The goal of the project is to develop a cohesive brand identity that conveys trustworthiness, inclusivity, and empowerment for veterans and their families.
After conducting stakeholder interviews that addressed the organization's objectives, brand goals, and the emotional themes significant to both the organization and its community, the aim was to develop a fresh, trustworthy, and inspiring brand.

This brand would honor military service and sacrifice, promote a sense of adventure, and expand No Salt Sailing's presence across both physical and digital platforms.
Phase one:
Began with a logo redesign that captured the essence of sailing and service. The logo needed to be simple, scalable, and memorable.

Additionally, it had to be versatile for use across various mediums, including embroidery, digital platforms, print, and merchandise.

No Salt Sailing is situated along the shores of Lake Michigan in Chicago, at Montrose Harbor and the Chicago Corinthian Yacht Club.

Phase three:
A scalable design system was developed to facilitate consistent brand usage and expansion, utilizing Figma. The system specified:

  • Typography Styles
  • Color Palette
  • UI Elements
  • Spacing & Grid Systems

Phase four:
Brand Application Examples Apparel Mockups:

  • Crew Shirts
  • Drinkware
  • Hats and Bags

About

Chad King is a UX designer and U.S. Army veteran focused on creating accessible, inclusive, and meaningful digital experiences. Drawing from his leadership experience and lived service, Chad approaches design challenges with empathy, structure, and a focus on real-world impact.

He is currently pursuing a master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction, with research interests that include exploring assistive technology for independent living and utilizing artificial intelligence to help veterans and their families access VA housing benefits and resources. This work reflects his commitment to using technology to promote independence, dignity, and well-being.

Chad possesses a diverse skill set that includes qualitative research, journey mapping, wire framing, visual design and prototyping. He is proficient in Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and front-end development technologies. He fosters cross-functional teamwork and ensures user needs remain central throughout the design process.

Outside of design, Chad is an avid sailor, food pantry volunteer, and active member of Chicago’s tech community. He believes great design meets people where they are and helps them move forward with dignity.

Museums serve as cultural and educational anchors in our communities, yet recent years have seen declining attendance due to factors like the pandemic and competition from other leisure activities.

How could technology re-engage audiences and boost museum visits

DISCOVαr proposes an augmented reality (AR) solution to transform static museum visits into personalized, immersive journeys. By combining customizable itineraries, AR-enhanced exhibits, and seamless navigation, DISCOVαr aims to not only improve the visitor experience but also increase time spent at museums, repeat visits, and overall engagement.
Problem Statement

Without a steady stream of visitors, museums risk losing vital revenue and public relevance. Traditional methods of attracting visitors have become less effective, especially among tech-savvy audiences.
Challenge

How might we use AR technology to make museum visits more engaging, accessible, and memorable?
Hypothesis

We hypothesized that integrating AR into the museum experience would: Enhance user enjoyment Boost engagement and time spent in museums Increase overall museum attendance
 

1. Research and Ideation

Conducted secondary research on museum attendance trends. Studied mobile app usage preferences (74% of museum-goers interested in apps for enhanced experiences). Held a design charrette session to rapidly sketch features and contexts.


2. Prototyping

Low-fidelity prototypes for initial user testing (desktop and mobile). Mid-fidelity iterations based on feedback: Added clear labels to icons Included "swipe left" hints for ticket navigation Enlarged the "Start Journey" button for AR initiation. High-fidelity prototypes finalized for usability evaluation.

3. Testing and Evaluation

We tested four core tasks across mobile and desktop: Buy a ticket (desktop) Retrieve and scan ticket (mobile) Start AR-guided journey (mobile) Leave a review (desktop)


Participants

Local parents, tourists, students, and tech enthusiasts.

Key Findings:

Participants were excited about AR integration ("like Pokémon GO for museums"). Struggles included unclear icons and confusing ticket retrieval. AR was identified as the most enjoyable part of the experience by multiple users.

 

Outcome

Positive validation of the hypothesis: AR features boosted engagement and museum visit intentions. Identified improvements for usability: clearer icons, event search enhancements, better ticket UI. Demonstrated potential for a scalable solution to address museum attendance challenges.



Next Steps

Further accessibility enhancements for all user groups. Expand AR content to include mini-games, quizzes, and interactive storytelling. Partner with museums to pilot DISCOVαr in a real-world setting.
